Policies and Procedures

Return Policies

Customers unhappy with their art products within 15 days of purchasing can receive a full refund as long as the product is not damaged, postage at buyer's expense.

If the product arrives damagedafter order online, the customer must meet all return requirements such as proof of purchase, the artwork will need to be inspected with photo evidence to proceed the exchange for a new artwork or gallery credit.

Procedures

When an artwork is purchased with specific requirements, it will come with additional notes to maintain artworks quailty and health. If the customer does not follow these requirements or maintain artwork. The artwork will not be refunded.
